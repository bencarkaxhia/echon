## OUR FAMILY VALUES

### 1. RESPECT EVERYONE'S PRIVACY

✓ What's shared in our family space stays in our family space
✓ Don't screenshot or share family content outside Echon without permission
✓ Don't post photos of living family members without asking them first
✓ If someone asks you to remove something, remove it respectfully

### 2. BE KIND, EVEN WHEN YOU DISAGREE

✓ Family members may have different memories or perspectives - that's okay
✓ If you have a conflict with someone, talk to them privately first
✓ Avoid bringing old family drama into the space
✓ Treat elders with extra patience and respect

### 3. PROTECT THE CHILDREN

✓ Don't share identifying information about children under 18
✓ Don't post photos of other people's children without parent permission
✓ Keep conversations age-appropriate

### 4. SHARE HONESTLY, NOT PERFECTLY

✓ It's okay if you don't know exact dates or names
✓ Mark things as "unsure" or "I think" when you're not certain
✓ Let others add their knowledge - we build this together
✓ Oral history and memory are as valuable as documents

### 5. YOU CONTROL YOUR OWN CONTRIBUTIONS

✓ You can edit or delete anything you've posted
✓ You can leave the space anytime and take your content with you
✓ You decide who sees what you share (everyone / specific people / private)

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### PRIVACY & SECURITY

**Q: Is this really private? Can strangers see our photos?**

A: Yes, it's completely private. Only people who are invited by existing family members can join. Nothing is searchable on Google or visible to non-members. Echon doesn't sell your data or show ads.

**Q: What if I accidentally share something I shouldn't have?**

A: You can delete it immediately. Click the "..." menu on any post you made and select "Delete." If someone else posted it, contact them or a founder.

**Q: Can Echon employees see our family photos/stories?**

A: Echon staff can only access your space if you explicitly request technical support and give permission. We never browse family spaces without consent. All content is encrypted.

**Q: What happens to our space if we stop paying the subscription?**

A: Free tier: Your space continues with limits (50 members, 10GB storage).
If you were on paid tier and cancel: 30-day grace period to export all data, then space goes read-only (can view, can't add new content) until you resubscribe.

### JOINING & INVITING

**Q: Who can I invite to the Çarkaxhia Space?**

A: Anyone who is genuinely part of the Çarkaxhia or Çulaj family (by blood, marriage, or adoption). This includes:
- Direct relatives (parents, siblings, children)
- Extended family (aunts, uncles, cousins)
- In-laws (spouses of family members)
- Adopted family members
- Close family friends IF founders approve

**Q: Can I invite my spouse who has a different last name?**

A: Yes! Spouses are family. When inviting, just note the relationship (e.g., "My wife, Maria").

**Q: What if someone claims to be family but I don't know them?**

A: They enter as a "Guest" first. Two existing members must vouch for them. Founders review after 30 days. If confirmed, they become full members.

**Q: Can someone who left the family (divorce, estrangement) still be in the space?**

A: This is decided by the family, not Echon. Founders can remove anyone if there's good reason. We recommend: keep it about preserving history, not punishing people.

---

### USING THE SPACE

**Q: I uploaded a photo but I don't know who's in it. What do I do?**

A: Tag it as "Unknown person" and add a note asking: "Does anyone know who this is?" Other family members can help identify them in the comments.

**Q: Can I edit something I posted last year?**

A: Yes, click the "..." menu on your post and select "Edit." The original version is saved in history, so if someone quotes it, we can see what changed.

**Q: How do I save a copy of everything I've uploaded?**

A: Go to Settings → Export My Contributions. You'll get a ZIP file with all your photos, stories, and voice recordings.

**Q: I don't want everyone to see something - how do I make it private?**

A: When uploading, choose "Who can see this?" and select:
- Just me (private)
- Only specific people (choose from list)
- Everyone in the space

You can change this later by editing the post.

### ELDER-SPECIFIC QUESTIONS

**Q: My dad doesn't have a smartphone. Can he still participate?**

A: Yes! Options:
1. He can use a computer (web browser)
2. You can help him upload by phone (he tells you what to write/upload)
3. He can mail physical photos/letters to you, and you digitize them for him
4. [Future] Echon Elder Help Line - we guide him by phone

**Q: My mom has trouble seeing small text. Can we make it bigger?**

A: Yes! In Settings → Accessibility:
- Large text mode
- High contrast mode
- Voice readout (phone reads posts aloud)

**Q: How do I help my grandmother who doesn't understand technology at all?**

A: See the "Elder Onboarding Guide" document. Key tips:
- Sit with her the first time
- Show her the voice recording button (no typing needed)
- Set her to "Simplified Mode" (fewer buttons)
- Offer to handle uploads for her

### MEMORIES & ORGANIZATION

**Q: We have hundreds of old photos. How do we organize them?**

A: Upload in batches. Echon auto-organizes by:
- Date/decade (if you provide it)
- Location (if you tag it)
- People (if you tag faces)

Don't worry about perfection - just get them in. You can always add details later.

**Q: What if two people remember the same event differently?**

A: Both memories are valid! Echon shows "Multiple accounts of this event." Users can see both versions. No one deletes the other's memory.

**Q: How far back can we trace our family?**

A: As far as you have information! Some families go back 5 generations, others trace to the 1600s through church records. It depends on what documents/memories survive.

**Q: Can we connect our space with another family's space (like if cousins marry)?**

A: Not yet, but we're working on "Allied Spaces" feature. For now, someone can be a member of both spaces separately (e.g., your daughter joins both her dad's and mom's family spaces).

### TECHNICAL QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I need to download an app?**

A: No, Echon works in your phone or computer's web browser. There's an optional app for iOS and Android if you prefer, but browser works fine.

**Q: How much storage do we get?**

A: Free tier: 10 GB (about 5,000 photos)
Family tier: Unlimited

**Q: What file types can I upload?**

Photos: JPG, PNG, HEIC
Videos: MP4, MOV (up to 10 min)
Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT
Audio: MP3, M4A, WAV

**Q: Can I upload videos?**

A: Yes, up to 10 minutes each. For longer videos (like wedding footage), we recommend YouTube unlisted link + sharing the link in Echon.

**Q: Does Echon work offline?**

A: You need internet to view/upload. However, if you're in a place with no internet, you can draft posts offline and they'll upload automatically when you reconnect.

### AFTER SOMEONE PASSES AWAY

**Q: What happens to my dad's contributions if he passes away?**

A: His posts remain in the space permanently unless:
1. He designated someone to inherit his account (they can manage it)
2. Founders decide to create a "Memorial Page" for him
3. Family votes to make his content extra-protected (view-only, no edits)

**Q: Can I create a page for relatives who died before Echon existed?**

A: Yes! Click "Add a Family Member" and select "Memorial Profile." You can:
- Upload their photos
- Share stories about them
- Collect memories from others who knew them

**Q: What if I die? Who controls my Echon contributions?**

A: In Settings → Account → Digital Legacy:
- Designate an heir (spouse, child, sibling)
- They get access to your account to:
  - Preserve your content
  - Download it
  - But can't edit/delete (your voice stays yours)

### PROBLEMS & SUPPORT

**Q: Something isn't working. Who do I contact?**

A: In order of speed:
1. Ask in your family space - another member might know
2. Check Settings → Help Center (FAQ + tutorials)
3. Email: support@echon.app
4. For paid tier: Call the help line (we guide you by phone)

**Q: I forgot my password.**

A: Click "Forgot Password" on login screen. We'll send a reset link to your email or SMS.

**Q: Someone is harassing me in the space. What do I do?**

A: 
1. Report them (click their profile → Report)
2. Founders are notified immediately
3. You can block them temporarily (they can't see your posts until founders resolve it)
4. For serious threats, contact local authorities - Echon will cooperate with law enforcement

**Q: Can I delete my account and all my data?**

A: Yes. Settings → Delete Account.
Warning: This removes ALL your contributions from the family space. Consider:
- Exporting your data first (you keep a copy)
- Just leaving the space (content stays, you're no longer active)
