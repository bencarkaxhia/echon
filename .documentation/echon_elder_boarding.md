## STEP 3: THE PHONE CALL WALKTHROUGH

### Script for Helping Elders Join (Phone Call)

**YOU:** "Dad, a ke celularin tënd afër?" / "Dad, do you have your phone near you?"

**THEM:** "Po" / "Yes"

**YOU:** "Shko tek WhatsApp dhe shiko mesazhet e mia. A i sheh?" / "Go to WhatsApp and look at my messages. Do you see them?"

**THEM:** "Po, po." / "Yes, yes."

**YOU:** "Shiko linkun që të dërgova - atë që është blu dhe me vija poshtë. Kliko atje." / "Look at the link I sent - the one that's blue and underlined. Click there."

**[They click the link]**

**YOU:** "Çfarë del në ekran? A thotë 'Welcome, Masar'?" / "What appears on screen? Does it say 'Welcome, Masar'?"

**THEM:** "Po, e shoh." / "Yes, I see it."

**YOU:** "Perfekt! Tani kliko butonin që thotë 'Continue' ose 'Vazhdo'."

**[They click]**

**YOU:** "Tani pyet disa pyetje të thjeshta. Pyetja e parë: Ku je lindur?"

**THEM:** "Në Gjakovë."

**YOU:** "Saktë. Shkruaj 'Gjakova' në kuti dhe kliko 'Continue'."

**[Continue guiding through each step]**

**YOU:** "Më në fund, pyet: 'Një kujtim që do të ndash?' Nuk është e detyrueshme, por mund të shkruash diçka të shkurtër, ose mund ta thuash me zë nëse dëshiron."

**THEM:** "Si ta them me zë?"

**YOU:** "Ka një buton të kuq që thotë 'Record'. E shikon?"

**THEM:** "Po."

**YOU:** "Shtyp dhe mbaj atë dhe fol. Kur mbarojsh, lëshoje. Aq është!"

**[Recording done]**

**YOU:** "E dëgjove? A tingëllon mirë?"

**THEM:** "Po, mirë është."

**YOU:** "Kliko 'Share with Family' dhe ke mbaruar! Tani je brenda hapësirës së Çarkaxhisë. Të gjithë ne jemi këtu."

## TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON ELDER ISSUES

### Issue 1: "I don't know how to click the link"

**SOLUTION (Phone):**
"Dad, put your finger on the blue text and hold it for one second. A menu will appear. Click 'Open' or 'Hap'."

**SOLUTION (Viber):**
"The blue text is a button. Just tap it once with your finger, like when you tap to call someone."

---

### Issue 2: "I don't see the app / it asks me to download something"

**SOLUTION:**
"Don't download anything yet. The link should open in your phone's browser - like when you search on Google. If it's asking to download, press 'Cancel' and send me a screenshot. I'll help you."

**[Note: Echon works in browser first, app download is optional]**

---

### Issue 3: "I'm afraid to click the wrong thing"

**SOLUTION:**
"Dad, don't worry. You can't break anything. If you click the wrong button, just go back. There's always a back arrow at the top. Try it - click anything, and if you don't like it, click the arrow to go back."

---

### Issue 4: "I don't know how to type on the phone"

**SOLUTION:**
"You don't have to type! See the big red button that says 'RECORD'? That's for you. Just press it and talk. The phone writes down what you say. Or I can help you type when we're on the phone together."

---

### Issue 5: "I don't have internet / it's not working"

**SOLUTION:**
"Check if you see the WiFi symbol at the top of your phone (looks like a fan 📶). If not, make sure you're connected to home WiFi. Go to Settings → WiFi → Pick your network."

**[If they're using mobile data]:**
"Make sure you have mobile data on. It's the same way you use WhatsApp or Viber - Echon uses the same internet."

## BEST PRACTICES FOR INVITING ELDERS

### ✅ DO:

1. **Send personal message first** (not just the link)
2. **Offer to help by phone** (don't expect them to figure it out alone)
3. **Use their language** (Albanian for Masar, not just English)
4. **Explain what they'll see** before they click
5. **Be patient** (expect to repeat instructions)
6. **Celebrate when they join** (make them feel welcomed, not like a burden)

### ❌ DON'T:

1. **Send just a link** with no context
2. **Assume they know how to click links** or navigate apps
3. **Get frustrated** if they don't understand immediately
4. **Use tech jargon** ("open your browser," "click the URL")
5. **Rush them** ("just click it Dad, it's easy")
6. **Make them feel stupid** for asking questions
